### Immersive Discussion Labs

Where PMO leaders go beyond surface-level conversations.

The Immersive Discussion Lab is a dedicated space for profound, high-impact dialogue — a room designed for PMO leaders who want to explore ideas that truly matter. No slides. No speeches. No formal presentations. Just meaningful conversations guided by compelling questions, real challenges, and the collective intelligence of the group.

Inside the Lab, participants dive into the issues that shape modern PMOs: strategic alignment, value creation, organizational dynamics, decision velocity, leadership, culture, and the evolving role of PMOs in complex environments such as capital and giga projects.

This is not a traditional discussion. It's an immersive experience where every voice matters, perspectives collide constructively, and insights emerge organically. You leave the room not with answers, but with clarity, new angles, deeper questions, and the energy to rethink what a PMO can truly become.

The Immersive Discussion Lab is where PMO thinking evolves.

### PMO Voices Panels

Where real PMOs step into the spotlight — and real stories shape real insight.

The PMO Voices Panels bring together PMO leaders from different industries, regions, and backgrounds to share the practices, decisions, struggles, and breakthroughs that truly define modern PMOs. These are not scripted presentations — they are authentic, experience-driven conversations that reveal what actually works in the real world.

Each panel places PMO professionals in the spotlight, inviting them to unpack their journeys and reflect on the lessons learned along the way. Through dynamic dialogue, contrasting perspectives, and honest storytelling, participants gain a richer understanding of how PMOs create value, influence strategy, navigate complexity, and enable outcomes across organizations.

In the PMO Voices Panels, real stories meet real dialogue, transforming lived experience into actionable insights. You walk away with ideas you can apply immediately — and inspiration you didn't know you needed.

This is where PMO voices resonate — and where the future of the profession takes shape.

### PMO unCON Voices Podcast

A Live Podcast Experience — and yes… you can be part of it.

At PMO unCON, every attendee brings a voice worth hearing. This year, we are opening a dedicated live podcast studio inside the event — capturing raw, authentic, and energizing conversations with PMO leaders and practitioners from around the world.

We want to hear: your perspective, your story, your challenges, your insights, and your unCON experience.

Throughout the two days of the event, we'll be recording short, spontaneous conversations that reflect the essence of the unCON: human, unconventional, collaborative, and deeply meaningful.

After the event, all attendees will receive full access to the complete podcast collection — every conversation recorded during the Summit.

If you'd like to be considered as a guest on the podcast, simply complete the application form and indicate your preferred recording times. Our team will review all submissions and send invitations to selected participants ahead of the event.

Your voice matters. Let's capture it at PMO unCON Voices Podcast.

Each session is crafted to be concise, actionable, and directly relevant to the realities PMO leaders face every day.

It's your opportunity to discover what's new, what's working, and what can make a real difference in your PMO — all in an energetic, engaging environment.

The PMO Solutions Stage turns insight into capability, one focused session at a time.

### Most Influential PMOs Ceremony

Witness the inaugural announcement of PMOs that demonstrate the strongest influence in North America.

The Most Influential PMOs Ceremony takes place at the end of Day 1 (March 24), during the PMO Community Reception — a highlight moment to celebrate PMOs that demonstrate the strongest, most consistent, and most positive influence in North America. This is not a competition — it's a journey of progressive trust and sustained excellence.

The ceremony recognizes PMOs across four stages of influence: Emerging Influence (initial visibility), Consistent Influence (relevance confirmed through consecutive validations), Sustained Influence (proven resilience across organizational changes), and Legacy Influence (influence embedded in the organization's DNA).

Influence is not a moment — it is a continuum. This program builds a repository of influence that transcends short-term recognition. Join us as we honor the PMOs shaping the future of the profession.

The Most Influential PMOs Ceremony is where excellence meets celebration.
