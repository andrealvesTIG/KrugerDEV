1. Variance columns in the Total block

After ETC, add three compact roll-up columns: Var $, Var %, Status.
Two variances configurable via a small toggle next to the Financial Type chips:
Forecast accuracy: ACT − FCST (YTD only)
Budget health: EAC − AOP where EAC = ACT_YTD + ETC
Status pill: ✓ On Track (|Var %| ≤ 5), ▲ At Risk (5–15%), ● Over (>15%), with matching colors.
2. Conditional cell coloring (per-month + total)

ACT cells turn amber when they exceed the same month's FCST by >10%, red when >25%; green when meaningfully under.
FCST cells turn amber/red when they drift from AOP by the same thresholds.
A subtle horizontal heat-bar behind each month's ACT cell, scaled to the row's max month, so spikes and dips pop visually.
Past months get a faint gray wash, future months stay crisp — easy "where are we" cue.
3. Row-level health indicator

A 4-px left accent bar on category and item rows colored by overall status (green/amber/red), so issues are scannable even when collapsed.
Rows where ETC > remaining AOP budget (i.e., forecast overrun) get a small ⚠︎ next to the cost item name with a tooltip explaining the variance.
4. Insights strip above the grid
A single horizontal band of 4–5 KPI tiles, fed by the same totals already computed:

EAC vs AOP — dollar variance + % with up/down arrow.
YTD Forecast accuracy — 1 − |ACT−FCST|/FCST, shown as %.
Burn rate — average monthly ACT over the last 3 months, with trend arrow vs. prior 3 months.
Months of runway at current burn — (AOP − ACT_YTD) / avg_burn.
Top variance driver — the single cost item with the largest absolute Var $, click-to-scroll.
5. Grand-total row gets the same treatment

Variance columns and status pill at the project level so the bottom line summarizes everything above.
6. Settings

Reuse the existing toolbar area: small "Insights" toggle (show/hide the strip) and a "Color rules" popover to tweak the 10% / 25% thresholds. Persist in the same place fiscal-year preference lives.
